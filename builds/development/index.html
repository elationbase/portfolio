The epicenter for cognitive training

The Brain Performance Institute was conceived by visionaries at UT Dallas, leaders of the University’s Center for BrainHealth, cognitive neuroscience experts, research clinicians and community advocates to address diminishing cognitive brainpower. Scientific discoveries can take 20 to 40 years to trickle down to meaningfully benefit human life. The Brain Performance Institute will serve as the premier model for the translation of rapidly emerging scientific brain discoveries to build and regain healthy brain function as quickly as possible.
